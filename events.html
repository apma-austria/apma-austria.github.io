APMA Junior Board

Representing and supporting young scientists in Proteomics, Lipidomics, Metabolomics and Bioinformatics.

Events

The APMA Junior Board also organizes events every now and then, mostly in the form of casual get togethers for meeting other young scientists and APMA members which focus on socializing and networking. We call these get togethers "APMA Stammisch" and we usually host them several times a year. Below you can find information about upcoming events that we are either directly involved in or that we endorse, as well as recaps of past events. For an exhaustive list of conferences and events in the -omics fields, please check our event calendar.
