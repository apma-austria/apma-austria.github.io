APMA Stammtisch Autumn 2023

The gathering of the APMA Junior Board in Salzburg was a resounding success, bringing together 14 participants from 6 different university locations. Following a highly productive board meeting at the charming Raschhofer's Rossbräu, we decided to host a quiz at the Huber working group's premises. To our surprise, no clear winner emerged, as every participant scored the same number of points, showcasing the impressively even distribution of analytical knowledge among APMA's junior members.

Unfortunately, the unpredictable Salzburg rain dashed our plans to visit the Christmas market. Nevertheless, we made the most of the situation by indulging in mulled wine, beer, and snacks, courtesy of the generous support and funding from APMA's networking representative, Karl Mechtler.

APMA Junior Board members enjoying a delightful meal at Raschhofer's Rossbräu before delving deep into discussion of urgent APMA-related topics. All Winners! Quiz attendees proudly display their score sheets in triumph.
